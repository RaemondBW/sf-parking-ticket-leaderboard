Source & method: SFMTA Parking Citations & Fines, DataSF open data portal, queried July 11, 2026 (23,900,814 citations, Jan 2008 – Jun 2026). Totals are fine amounts as issued, not amounts paid. Rankings aggregate by license plate as published in the dataset; ~62,000 citations with no recorded plate ($6.6M) are excluded, as are 4 records mis-dated to 2027. A plate can change vehicles or owners over 18 years, so a plate's total is not strictly one vehicle's total. Overall ranks compare total fines across all 5,675,758 ticketed plates; a rank marked ≈ is an instant estimate that refines to the exact figure once the live count returns.
